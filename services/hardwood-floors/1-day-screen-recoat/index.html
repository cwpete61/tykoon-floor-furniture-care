What is 1-Day Screen & Recoat?

Our 1-day screen and recoat service is the perfect solution for hardwood floors showing signs of light wear, such as dull finish or minor scratches. Unlike full sanding, this process involves lightly screening the surface to remove the top layer of existing finish, then applying a fresh coat of durable, eco-friendly polyurethane. The result is a rejuvenated floor with restored shine and protection, completed in a single day to minimize disruption to your daily routine.

Ideal for residential homes in Beverly Hills, Santa Monica, and other LA neighborhoods, as well as commercial spaces like offices and boutiques. This maintenance service extends the life of your hardwood floors, preventing the need for more invasive refinishing for years.
