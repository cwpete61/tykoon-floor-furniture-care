Full Sanding & Refinishing Services

Complete hardwood floor restoration through professional sanding and refinishing. Custom staining and durable finishes for heavily worn floors.

What is Full Sanding & Refinishing?

Full sanding and refinishing is the most comprehensive method for restoring hardwood floors. This process removes all existing finish, imperfections, and a thin layer of wood to create a completely smooth surface ready for custom staining and a new protective finish. It's ideal for floors with significant wear, including deep scratches, water damage, gum, or pet stains that can't be addressed with lighter maintenance services.

Our dustless sanding technology minimizes mess and airborne particles, making it suitable for occupied homes and businesses in Los Angeles. We sand to the bare wood, apply your choice of premium stain, and finish with multiple coats of high-durability polyurethane. The result is a like-new hardwood floor that can last for years without needing additional work.

Service Details

  • • Dustless sanding to bare wood with professional equipment
  • • Custom stain application in multiple colors and tones
  • • Multiple coats of low-VOC polyurethane (matte, satin, or gloss)
  • • 2-3 day completion for most residential projects
  • • Complete preparation and cleanup included

Benefits of Full Sanding & Refinishing

This service transforms old, damaged hardwood floors into beautiful, durable surfaces that increase your property value. By removing all imperfections and applying a fresh finish, your floors look and perform like new. It's the best choice for antique or vintage hardwood floors, as well as modern installations that have seen heavy use over the years.

Serving Los Angeles neighborhoods from Beverly Hills to Koreatown, our full refinishing service is perfect for luxury homes, apartments, offices, and retail spaces. We use premium materials and techniques that ensure long-lasting results with minimal disruption to your schedule.
