Gym Floor Refinishing Specialized Process

Our gym floor refinishing service is specifically designed for high-traffic sports flooring in basketball courts, volleyball courts, and fitness centers. We understand the unique demands placed on gym floors and use specialized equipment and materials to restore safety, performance, and appearance without disrupting athletic schedules.

Unlike residential hardwood floor refinishing, gym floor work requires faster drying times, enhanced durability, and specific surface characteristics that perform well under athletic use. Our team specializes in working during off-hours to minimize impact on gym operations while delivering premium results.

Gym Floor Refinishing Process

  • • Surface preparation with gym-safe dustless sanding equipment
  • • Repair of damage from athletic equipment and high-impact activities
  • • Application of gymnasium-grade polyurethane finish systems
  • • Ultra-violet stable coatings resistant to fading from gym lighting
  • • Optional slip-resistant surface treatments for safety
  • • Multiple finish options: matte, satin, or gloss finish systems
