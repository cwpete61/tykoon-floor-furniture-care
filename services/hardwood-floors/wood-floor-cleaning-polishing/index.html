Deep Cleaning & Polishing Process

Our comprehensive wood floor cleaning and polishing service combines deep cleaning with protective coating to restore your hardwood floors to their optimal condition. We remove built-up dirt, soil, and minor imperfections while applying a fresh layer of protective finish that enhances the natural beauty of your wood and provides added durability against everyday wear.

This service is perfect for floors that have lost their shine due to daily use, need refresh before special events, or require maintenance between more extensive refinishing work. Our mild, wood-safe cleaning solutions ensure the beauty and integrity of your investment are preserved.

Our Cleaning Process

  • • Initial assessment and protective masking of furniture and baseboards
  • • Deep cleaning using wood-safe, pH-neutral detergents
  • • Gentle scrubbing to remove embedded dirt and light stains
  • • Rinse with demineralized water to prevent water spots
  • • Professional drying and buffing with soft microfiber cloths
  • • Optional protective coating application for extra shine and durability

Polishing Benefits

Our polishing process doesn't just clean - it enhances and protects your hardwood floors. The application of our signature finishing polymer adds multiple benefits beyond simple cleaning, providing enhanced protection against future dirt and wear while bringing out the depth and richness of your wood's natural grain.

Serving clients from Hollywood Hills to Pasadena, our cleaning and polishing services are tailored to each floor type and condition. Whether you need a one-time deep clean, regular maintenance, or interim care between major refinishing projects, we deliver results that exceed expectations.
