Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my tile and grout professionally cleaned?

For residential properties, we recommend professional cleaning every 12-18 months, depending on traffic and usage. High-traffic commercial areas may require cleaning every 6-12 months to maintain appearance and hygiene standards.

Is sealing necessary after cleaning?

We strongly recommend sealing grout lines and porous tiles after cleaning. Sealing protects against stains, water damage, and bacterial growth, extending the life and appearance of your surfaces. Our commercial-grade sealers provide long-lasting protection against everyday wear.

How long does concrete polishing last?

Properly maintained polished concrete can last for decades. The initial polish typically lasts 5-10 years before requiring maintenance, depending on traffic levels. Regular cleaning and occasional resealing will maintain the appearance and performance of your polished concrete floors.

Do you work with all types of tile?

Yes, we have experience with virtually all tile materials including ceramic, porcelain, natural stone (marble, granite, travertine, slate), terra cotta, quarry tile, saltillo, and more. Each surface requires specific techniques and products, which our technicians are fully trained to apply.
