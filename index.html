Our Services

Professional floor and furniture care solutions for your home or business

1-Day Screen & Recoat

Fast, affordable maintenance for lightly worn hardwood floors. Complete in just one day with eco-friendly finishes.

Full Sanding & Refinishing

Complete restoration for heavily worn floors. Professional sanding and custom staining for a like-new finish.

Cleaning & Polishing

Professional deep cleaning and polishing to remove dirt, scratches, and restore shine to your hardwood floors.

Gym Floor Refinishing

Specialized refinishing for sports floors. Durable, high-performance finishes for basketball courts and fitness centers.

Fabric Upholstery Cleaning

Deep cleaning for sofas, chairs, and all fabric upholstery. Removes dirt, stains, and allergens with eco-friendly methods.

Leather Furniture Restoration

Complete leather care including cleaning, conditioning, color restoration, and protection for all leather furniture.
