The Complete Guide to Hardwood Floor Maintenance

Published on October 7, 2025

Maintaining hardwood floors requires a comprehensive approach to preserve their beauty and value. Daily care involves regular sweeping and vacuuming, while weekly damp-mopping with specialized cleaners ensures deeper cleaning. Preventive measures like entrance mats, furniture pads, and controlled indoor humidity (35-55%) are essential for long-term protection. Quick response to spills and proper UV protection help prevent damage. Professional maintenance, including annual deep cleaning and refinishing every 3-5 years, is crucial for extending floor life. Watch for warning signs like visible scratches or water damage spots that indicate the need for professional intervention.
